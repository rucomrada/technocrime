Our Security Services

Comprehensive security solutions tailored to your needs

Manned Guarding

The company offers guarding services to residential and commercial premises including factories, shops, supermarkets, filling stations, institutions and firms.

Our commitment is to ensure that we provide practical solutions to the ever-changing customer needs through well trained guards and guardettes.

Our security guard team is equipped with highly-trained security guards who have the capability to deter incidents before they happen and quickly respond in a professional manner, when necessary. Our security guards perform a variety of tasks that are specifically designed to meet a client's unique security needs in order to protect their valuable property and assets.

Dog Handlers and Training

Professional K9 security services for enhanced protection and threat detection.

The common security dogs for patrols among the breeds include: German shepherd, Doberman, sheepdog, and Rottweiler.

  • Trained security dogs and professional handlers
  • Breeding services working with qualified veterinary officers
  • Aggressive and obedience training programs
  • Enhanced security patrols and perimeter protection
  • Threat detection and deterrence

Technology Solutions

Modern security technology for comprehensive protection.

Electric Fencing

We are the market leader in electric fencing in the region. Our products, expertise and distribution network ensure you obtain the best electric fence for your premises. Electric fencing can provide safe and secure boundaries for permanent, semi-permanent or temporary applications at a cost lower than conventional fences.

  • CCTV installation and monitoring
  • Electric fence design and installation
  • Alarm systems
  • Access control systems

Fire Management

Comprehensive fire safety solutions and training services.
