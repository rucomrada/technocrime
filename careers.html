Recruitment Requirements

Required Documents

  • Certificate of Good Conduct
  • Letter from previous employer
  • National Identity Card (ID)
  • Recent two colored passport photos
  • K.C.S.E Certificate and above
  • Physical and Mental fitness (Health Report)
  • Letter from area chief
  • Introduction letter from two referees with their contacts and ID photocopies

Training Process

We have introduced a proper training system synonymous with quality professionalism and customer satisfaction. Despite any previous training or experience, any recruit is subjected to attend training for a period of one month.

The recruits are vetted properly to ensure we maintain the highest standards of security personnel. To improve their performance, the recruits are placed under a training process that is continuous to ensure alertness and promptness round the clock.

Training Duration: 1 Month
