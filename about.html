About Technocrime Protective Services

Leading the way in professional security solutions across Kenya

Who We Are

TECHNOCRIME PROTECTIVE SERVICES is a security services provider with a team of highly trained security officers and specialists.

Technocrime has a security division that offers a wide range of security services to businesses and residential communities looking for secure and cost-effective security solutions.

All our field supervisors undergo training in law enforcement and people management. Additionally, most of our staff are former law enforcement officers with the qualifications and expertise necessary to provide the highest level of protection possible for our clients.

This level of security expertise is unparalleled in the security industry, and it's what sets Technocrime apart from other security companies.
